## 30-Second Tutorial

### 1. Open the Builder
Visit http://localhost:3001/builder-v2

### 2. Add Your First Section (Hero)
- Look at the right panel "Section Library"
- Click **"Hero"** under Essentials
- A hero section appears in the canvas!

### 3. Customize It
- Click the hero section to select it
- See the **Variant Picker** appear (5 layouts to choose from)
- Click "Centered" for a classic portfolio look
- Edit your name, headline, and bio in the Inspector panel below

### 4. Add More Sections
Try adding:
- **Skills**: Show your expertise
- **Projects**: Display your work
- **GitHub**: Add your streak (just enter your GitHub username!)
- **Contact**: Make it easy to reach you

### 5. Reorder Sections
- Click a section to select it
- Use the **drag handle** (‚ãÆ‚ãÆ) that appears in the top-right
- Drag sections up or down

### 6. Preview & Share

- Your portfolio opens in a new tab

## Troubleshooting

### Sections not showing?
- Check the Section Library is open (right panel)
- Try searching for the section name

### Can't edit a section?
- Make sure it's selected (click it)
- Inspector panel will appear on the right

### Changes not saving?
- Check browser console for errors
- Clear localStorage and start fresh if needed

### GitHub widgets not loading?
- Check your username is correct
- Widgets load from external services (may take a moment)
- Check internet connection
